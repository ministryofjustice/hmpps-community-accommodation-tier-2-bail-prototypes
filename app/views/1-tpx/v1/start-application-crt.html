Apply for Bail Community Accommodation Services (CAS-2)

Use this service to apply for short-term accommodation and support on behalf of someone who has been granted bail.

Before you start

You’ll need to:

  • create an entry for the applicant on nDelius if they do not have one already
  • get consent from the applicant
{{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "How to create an entry on nDelius", text: "" }) }}

Supporting information

To submit the application, you’ll need details about:

  • their current or last known address
  • their preferred areas
  • if they need a CAS-2 family unit property
  • their offending history
  • their solicitor
  • their health needs
  • the risks of serious harm to themselves or others
  • how they are paying for CAS-2 accommodation
  • equality information
All questions are mandatory, unless marked as optional.
You can save your progress and return to the application at any time.
