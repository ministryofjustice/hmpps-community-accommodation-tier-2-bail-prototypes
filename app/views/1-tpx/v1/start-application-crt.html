Apply for Court Bail Community Accommodation Services (CAS-2)

Use this service to apply for temporary accommodation and support on behalf of someone who has been granted bail.

You can save your progress and return to the application at any time.

Before you start

You’ll need to create an entry for the applicant on nDelius if they do not have one already.

Supporting information

To start an application, you’ll need at least the following details about the applicant:

  • their current or last known address
  • information about their case, including the court name, the court hearing date, any known Case Reference Number and onward prison (if applicable)
  • their preferred areas
  • if they need a CAS-2 family unit

To submit the full application, you’ll also need to enter details on the applicant’s:

  • offending history
  • solicitor
  • health needs
  • risk of serious harm to themselves or others
  • temporary accommodation funding
  • equality monitoring information
All questions are mandatory, unless marked as optional.
