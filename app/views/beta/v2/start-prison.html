Apply for short-term accommodation (CAS2) for bail

Use this service to apply for short-term accommodation and support on behalf of someone who is seeking bail.

Before you start

To use this service, you’ll need:

  • verbal consent from the applicant to apply on their behalf
  • their prison number - this is the number for the applicant’s record in the Prison National Offender Management Information System (p-NOMIS) .

Supporting information

To submit the application, you’ll need details about:

  • their solicitor
  • their last known address
  • their equality information
  • their offending history
  • their health needs
  • the risks of serious harm to themselves or others
  • how they are paying for CAS-2 accommodation
  • their preferred areas and exclusion zones
  • their need for a CAS-2 family unity property
  • their bail, including any conditions and the hearing date
All questions are mandatory, unless marked as optional.
You can save your progress and return to the application at any time.
