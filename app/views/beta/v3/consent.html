What they must agree to for the application

They must understand that:

  • the application includes personal, private and legal information about them

  • their information will be used to decide if the accommodation is safe and suitable for them
  • their data will be collected and used as explained in the privacy notice (opens in a new tab)

What they must agree to if they move into the accommodation

They must understand that:

  • the accommodation is short-term and includes weekly support
  • they must pay for the accommodation themselves or using public funds like benefits
  • they must leave the accommodation when their licence period or bail order ends
  • they must find somewhere to live after they leave, with help from their support worker

How to get consent

  • Read or share this information with them.
  • Check they understand each point.
  • Ask them if they agree to the application and what they must do.
  • They must clearly say yes to give consent.
