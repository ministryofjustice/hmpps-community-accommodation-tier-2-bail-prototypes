Apply for short-term accommodation (CAS2)

Use this service to apply for short-term accommodation for people being released, recalled, bailed or leaving an approved premises with no suitable accommodation.

Who is eligible

The applicant must:

  • be 18 or over
  • be low or medium risk
  • not be serving a sentence of 4 or more years
  • not have any sexual offence allegations, charges, convictions or cautions
  • not have broken UK immigration laws (except overstaying)
  • not have ‘no recourse to public funds’

What you’ll need

You’ll need:

  • their CRN
  • verbal consent from the applicant

You’ll be asked for information about:

  • personal details
  • previous address
  • preferred areas and exclusion zones
  • funding and ID
  • offence details
  • previous unspent convictions
  • health needs
  • risks to self or others
  • bail conditions and hearing information
If you're applying from court, submit by 4pm for same-day assessment.
All questions are mandatory, unless marked as optional.
You can save your progress and return to the application at any time.
