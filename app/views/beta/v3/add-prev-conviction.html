Which types of convictions to add

Add any unspent convictions that meet one or more of the following criteria:

  • are relevant to the charges the applicant is being held for
  • relate to the following categories:
    • arson
    • domestic abuse
    • drugs
    • hate-related attitudes
    • stalking and harassment
    • violence
    • weapons and firearms
  • might raise concerns towards other adults and children
  • where the applicant is currently serving a sentence

Do not add any:

  • spent convictions
  • unspent convictions that do not meet any of the criteria listed
