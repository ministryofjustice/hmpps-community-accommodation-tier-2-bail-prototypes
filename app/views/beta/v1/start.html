Apply for Bail Community Accommodation Services (CAS-2)

Use this service to apply for short-term accommodation and support on behalf of someone who has been granted bail.

You can apply for applicants who are being held in court and in prison.

Before you start

For court applicants

You’ll need their Case Reference Number (CRN). If they do not have one already, you’ll need to create an entry for the applicant on the national probation database (nDelius).

{{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "How to create an entry on nDelius", text: "" }) }}

For prison applicants

You’ll need their prison number. This is the number for the applicant’s record in the Prison National Offender Management Information System (p-NOMIS).

{{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "Where to find the prison number", text: "" }) }}

Supporting information

To submit the application, you’ll need details about:

  • their last known address
  • their preferred areas
  • if they need a CAS-2 family unit property
  • their offending history
  • their solicitor
  • their health needs
  • the risks of serious harm to themselves or others
  • how they are paying for CAS-2 accommodation
  • their equality information

For prison applicants

We’ll also ask for information about their case, including the court name and the hearing date.

All questions are mandatory, unless marked as optional.
You can save your progress and return to the application at any time.
