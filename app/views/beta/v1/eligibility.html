Important

To apply for Bail Community Accommodation Services (CAS-2), the applicant must:

Be:

  • 18 years old or older
  • able to live independently in shared accommodation

Not:

  • be currently charged with, or have any past convictions of cautions, or current allegations of any sexual offences in Schedule 3 of the Sexual Offences Act 2003
  • be marked as at high or very high risk of serious harm on OASys (Offender Assessment System)
  • have breached immigration law (other than overstaying an approved period of leave to enter or remain in the UK)
  • have the ‘no recourse to public funds’ (NRPF) condition applied to their permission to enter or stay in the UK
